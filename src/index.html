About Me

I'm Lewis Campbell.

A maker who has set a goal to continuously build, write, develop and create. Documenting the journey of each, on the Making in Public channel and the Makers Focus podcast.

The Making in Public channel tells the story of each project. The ups and downs, trials and tribulations and let me tell you there are plenty of those. (This website alone had many trials)

Conversely. The Makers Focus podcast is an aside. A more detailed overview of why I approached the project the way I did and what I focused on to improve. Along with talking about my learnings from the experience to share with you.
